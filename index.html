Everything should be as simple as it can be...

Our aim is to deliver the possibility to get your purchased item anywhere and at anytime you want in the simplest way.

... but not simpler!

  1. We use data to predict a purchase in a foreseeable futur. It allow us to dispatch a list of eligible items in parcel drop off points or public lockers. So the client or the courier can get it as soon as the command as been made.
  2. It is important for us to minimize the hardness of the dispatch rider. To do so we use operation reseach and optimization tools to create the smartest way to deliver items in the most optimal style in real time.
